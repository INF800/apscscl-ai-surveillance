Our Intelligent Solutions

Automated Gunny Bag Logistics

Use Case: Revolutionize warehouse operations by accurately tracking gunny bags in real-time. Our system enhances inventory precision, reduces manual labor, and provides critical data for supply chain optimization.

Our Approach: Leveraging advanced object detection and instance segmentation, our AI model identifies, counts, and tracks gunny bags through complex logistical environments, integrating with inventory systems for seamless management.

See our system intelligently manage inventory flow.

Secure Face Recognition

Use Case: Elevate security standards with robust facial recognition for access control, identity verification, and watchlist monitoring across diverse operational scenarios.

Our Approach: Our deep learning models provide superior accuracy in face detection and matching, resilient to challenging conditions like poor lighting, varied poses, and partial occlusions. Designed for speed and scalability.

Smart Vehicle Authentication

Use Case: Automate and secure vehicle entry/exit points for facilities, campuses, and parking structures. Our system enhances traffic flow and logs vehicle movements with precision.

Our Approach: We combine high-accuracy License Plate Recognition (LPR) with vehicle make, model, and color identification (MMCR) for a multi-layered authentication process, ensuring robust and reliable access control.

Proactive Video Analytics

Use Case: Transform passive surveillance into active intelligence. Detect anomalies, monitor crowd dynamics, identify unattended objects, and analyze traffic patterns for enhanced security and operational efficiency.

Our Approach: A modular suite of AI-driven analytics tools that can be customized to detect specific events and behaviors, providing real-time alerts and actionable data for informed decision-making.
