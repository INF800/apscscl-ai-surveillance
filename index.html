About the Hackathon

The APSCSCL Smart Surveillance Hackathon (April 2025 Challenge) aimed to leverage cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques for advanced video analytics. The primary goal was to enhance security, efficiency, and monitoring capabilities within APSCSCL warehouses using existing CCTV infrastructure. Participants were challenged to develop innovative solutions for real-time object detection, recognition, and event analysis from video feeds.

Our Solution

Face Recognition

We implemented a robust face recognition system to identify authorized warehouse personnel. Our approach utilizes ArcFace, a state-of-the-art deep learning model for face feature extraction, combined with the InsightFace library for efficient implementation. This allows for accurate real-time identification, ensuring only permitted individuals access restricted areas and helps in tracking personnel movement for enhanced security and operational auditing.

Vehicle Recognition

For vehicle monitoring, our solution employs YOLOv8 for detecting vehicles in CCTV footage. Once a vehicle is detected, we perform license plate recognition using a combination of OCR techniques, primarily PaddleOCR (or alternatively OpenALPR). This enables automated logging of vehicle entry/exit, tracking vehicle movement within the premises, and identifying unauthorized vehicles, crucial for logistics management and security.
